Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

Find Friends is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our video chat service.

2. Information We Collect

Minimal Data Collection: We designed our platform to collect the minimum amount of data necessary for operation.

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: Temporarily logged for security and abuse prevention
  • Browser Information: Type, version, and operating system
  • Connection Data: Duration of connections and basic usage statistics
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for age verification and preferences

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Display Name: The name you choose to show others (max 30 characters)
  • Gender: Basic demographic information for matching preferences
  • Chat Messages: Text messages sent during conversations

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the video chat service
  • To match users based on gender preferences
  • To prevent abuse and ensure platform safety
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To improve our service through anonymous usage analytics

4. What We DON'T Do

Privacy Commitments:

  • We DO NOT record or store video conversations
  • We DO NOT permanently store chat messages
  • We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties
  • We DO NOT use tracking pixels or invasive analytics
  • We DO NOT create permanent profiles of users

5. Data Storage and Security

Important: Video and audio streams are transmitted peer-to-peer (P2P) and do not pass through our servers.

  • All data transmission uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/WSS)
  • WebRTC connections use end-to-end encryption
  • Temporary logs are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • IP addresses are only stored for abuse prevention

6. Cookies

We use minimal essential cookies:

  • Age Verification: Remembers that you confirmed you're 18+
  • Session Management: Maintains your chat session
  • Preferences: Remembers your display name and gender

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • STUN/TURN Servers: For WebRTC connectivity (Google, Metered.ca)
  • Content Delivery: For faster service delivery

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect minimal technical data.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about data collection practices
  • Request deletion of any stored personal data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Report privacy concerns

9. Data Retention

  • Chat Messages: Not permanently stored
  • Connection Logs: Deleted after 24 hours
  • IP Addresses: Deleted after 7 days (unless involved in abuse investigation)
  • Reports: Kept for 30 days for review

10. International Data Transfers

WebRTC connections may route through international servers. All transmissions remain encrypted and secure.

11. Children's Privacy

18+ Only: This service is strictly for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

13. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please use the reporting feature within the platform.

14. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing includes:

  • Consent (age verification and service agreement)
  • Legitimate interest (service operation and security)
  • Legal obligation (abuse prevention and compliance)
